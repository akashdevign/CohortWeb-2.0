PERSPECTIVE POKEMON CARD

Bulbasaur

For some time after its birth, it uses the nutrients that are packed into the seed on its back in order to grow.

Greninja

The movement of air against the long tongue Greninja keeps wrapped around its neck allows it to sense enemies it can’t see.

Reyquaza

Rayquaza is said to have lived for hundreds of millions of years. Legends remain of how it put to rest the clash between Kyogre and Groudon.

Arceus

According to the legends of Sinnoh, this Pokémon emerged from an egg and shaped all there is in this world.

Pikachu

When it is angered, it immediately discharges the energy stored in the pouches in its cheeks.

Lucario

It’s said that no foe can remain invisible to Lucario, since it can detect auras—even those of foes it could not otherwise see.

Reshirum

This legendary Pokémon can scorch the world with fire. It helps those who want to build a world of truth.

Mewtwo

Its DNA is almost the same as Mew’s. However, its size and disposition are vastly different.

Aerodactyl

This is a ferocious Pokémon from ancient times. Apparently even modern technology is incapable of producing a perfectly restored specimen.

Tapu Lele

It heals the wounds of people and Pokémon by sprinkling them with its sparkling scales. This guardian deity is worshiped on Akala.

Giratina

This Pokémon is said to live in a world on the reverse side of ours, where common knowledge is distorted and strange.
